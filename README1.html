Overview

TigerΔ is a bio-mimetic autonomous defense engine designed for high-entropy, contested environments. It treats hostile activity as physical energy and converts impact into resilience, memory, and adaptive mutation.

Core Principles

  • Mass–Energy Equivalence (E = mc²) — attack energy is converted into defensive mass.
  • Negative Radius — inversion state immune to linear pattern matching.
  • Scars Energy — accumulated experience from survived impacts.
  • Septachord Mutation — seven-phase non-periodic evolution.

Architecture

  • AtomicCore — nonlinear resonance & threat probability.
  • Lagrange Stabilizer — equilibrium of energy and mass.
  • Lumis Cycle — entropy respiration and rest states.
  • SimulUnit — digital twin & decoy generation.

Swarm & Autonomy

Designed for UAV / UGV swarms, degraded comms, and full autonomy. Operates even in isolation.
